NFL Teams

There are 32 teams in the NFL. These teams are broken into two conferences, AFC (American Football Conference) and NFC (National Football Conference). The AFC and NFC are then broken into smaller, regional divisions. There's AFC East, AFC North, AFC South, AFC West, NFC East, NFC North, NFC South and NFC West.
