NFL Teams

There are 32 teams in the NFL. These teams are broken into two conferences, AFC (American Football Conference) and NFC (National Football Conference). The AFC and NFC are then broken into smaller, regional divisions. There's AFC East, AFC North, AFC South, AFC West, NFC East, NFC North, NFC South and NFC West.

AFC East: New England Patriots, Miami Dolphins, Buffalo Bills and the New York Jets.

AFC North: Baltimore Ravens, Pittsburgh Steelers, Cincinnati Bengals and Cleveland Browns

AFC South: Houston Texans, Indianapolis Colts, Tennessee Titans and Jacksonville Jaguars

AFC West: Oakland Raiders, Kansas City Chiefs, Denver Broncos and San Diego Chargers

NFC East: Dallas Cowboys, New York Giants, Washington Redskins and Philadelphia Eagles

NFC North: Detroit Lions, Minnesota Vikings, Green Bay Packers and Chicago Bears

NFC South: Atlanta Falcons, Tampa Bay Buccaneers, New Orleans Saints and Carolina Panthers

NFC West: Seattle Seahawks, Arizona Cardinals, Los Angeles Rams and San Francisco 49ers
